# The World Beyond Bridgeport

Bridgeport sits at the mouth of the Godsfall River on the western coast 
of **Algar**, one nation among many on the continent of **Elderland**. 
It is a busy, prosperous city — and it is also a very small part of a 
very large world.

## The Continent of Elderland

Elderland is the known world for most of its inhabitants. It is vast 
enough that few people ever see more than a fraction of it in a 
lifetime, and large enough that its eastern reaches are more legend than 
geography to those living on the western coast.

Algar occupies a significant portion of Elderland's western reaches. It 
is a parliamentary monarchy, governed by a Crown whose authority extends 
across hereditary Duchies and Baronies — except in places like 
Bridgeport, whose Freehold status grants it unusual autonomy. The 
capital is **Knightsbridge**, far to the south.

Beyond Algar lie other nations, other peoples, and other histories. The 
continent has been shaped by centuries of conflict, migration, and 
the rise and fall of civilizations whose names survive only in the 
oldest archives.

## Beyond Elderland

The world does not end at Elderland's shores. Other continents lie 
across the seas, each with its own character:

**Issitania** — Scorched and sun-bound. A land of heat, endurance, and 
ancient power baked into the stone.

**Lakh-Ur** — Ancient and unyielding. Whatever shaped Lakh-Ur shaped it 
a very long time ago, and it has not forgotten.

**Saturna** — Distant and half-mythical. Few who speak of Saturna have 
been there. Whether the stories are accurate is difficult to verify.

Ships leave Bridgeport's harbor for distant ports. Some of those 
voyages eventually reach these shores. Most sailors have opinions about 
what they find there.

## The Mirror Planes

Beyond the material world lie two mirror realms — dimensions that 
reflect and refract Orb in different ways:

**The Umbral** — A realm of shadow, memory, and endings. The Umbral 
exists alongside the material world as its darker reflection, a place 
where boundaries blur and old truths linger. It is presided over by 
**Noctis**. Those who venture there find that the Umbral remembers 
things the material world has forgotten.

**Haelo** — A realm of growth, excess, and untamed possibility. Haelo 
is the fey mirror — wilder, stranger, and less bound by the rules that 
govern mortal existence. The goblins of the Gutterdeep Warrens are said 
to have slipped into the world through a fey crossing at Silmar, driven 
out of Haelo by forces they refuse to name clearly.

The boundaries between Orb and these mirror planes are not always 
stable. Thin places exist where the walls grow weak — and what lies on 
the other side does not always wait to be invited through.

## A Note on Scale

Most of this is background knowledge — the kind of thing a well-traveled 
merchant or a well-read scholar might know. The party's story begins in 
Bridgeport. The wider world exists, it presses in at the edges, and it 
will make itself felt in time.

For now, the Godsfall River is running, the harbor is busy, and there 
is work to be done.
