# Welcome to the Moonshroud Realms

This is the official campaign site for our Daggerheart campaign set in 
the Moonshroud Realms — a world created by Sandy Lawson and published 
by Grim Press.

Here you'll find lore, faction information, NPC profiles, session 
recaps, and anything else your GM wants you to know — or wants you to 
think you know.

The site grows as the campaign does. Pages will be added and expanded 
as the party discovers new people, places, and secrets. If something 
isn't here yet, the party probably hasn't found it yet.

---

## Leaving Comments & Reactions

Every page on this site has a comment section at the bottom. You can 
use it to ask questions, share theories, note things your character 
might be thinking, or just react to what you've read.

**To comment you'll need a free GitHub account:**

1. Go to **[github.com](https://github.com)** and sign up — it's free 
and takes about two minutes
2. Come back to any page on this site and scroll to the bottom
3. Click **Sign in with GitHub** and authorize Giscus when prompted — 
this is a one-time step
4. You're in — comment on any page from that point on

The emoji reactions at the top of each comment section work without 
typing anything — just click to react.
