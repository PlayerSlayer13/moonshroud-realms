# The World

The Moonshroud Realms is a world of ancient power and creeping decay — where the bones of 
civilization rest on foundations older than memory, and where something beneath the surface 
has begun, slowly and inexorably, to come apart.

## The Shape of the World

The known world centers on **Elderland**, a vast continent of diverse peoples, competing 
kingdoms, and deep wilderness. Within Elderland lies **Algar**, a country of merchant cities, 
old forests, and Crown authority that grows thinner the further one travels from the capital.

On Algar's western coast, where the **Godsfall River** meets **Pell's Harbor**, sits 
**Bridgeport** — a freehold city of some 8,000 souls, and the place where this story begins.

## The Peoples of the Realms

The Moonshroud Realms is home to many peoples. Among those the party has encountered:

- The **Aelfaen** — a people with deep ties to the old forests, found in great numbers in 
the woodland settlements south of Bridgeport
- The **Ursinor** — bear-folk of the deep woodlands, organized by clan and governed by 
ancient tradition
- Humans, who are everywhere, in every role, at every level of society

## The Feeling of the World

This is not a world of clear heroes and obvious villains. Power is held by those who can 
keep it, justice is local and imperfect, and the old stories are not always wrong. Magic 
is real but not commonplace — it draws attention, carries cost, and is rarely as simple 
as it appears.

Something is wrong with the world at a fundamental level. The party may not know this yet. 
But they will.
