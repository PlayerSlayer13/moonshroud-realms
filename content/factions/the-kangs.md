# The Kangs

Bridgeport has neighborhoods where the city's official rules are more 
suggestion than law. In those places, other arrangements apply.

The Kangs are a presence in certain parts of the city. Anyone who spends 
time on Bridgeport's streets will notice them eventually — groups moving 
with purpose, wearing a single item in a consistent color. Red. Blue. 
Yellow.

What that means, exactly, is the kind of thing most people in Bridgeport 
have learned not to ask too loudly.

They are not the city guard. They are not the Council. They have their 
own territory, their own rules, and their own way of making those rules 
felt. Most residents have arrived at a comfortable working arrangement 
of mutual non-interference.

Whether that's wisdom or just good sense probably depends on who you ask.
