# Calendar of Orb

The standard calendar of the Moonshroud Realms uses 13 months of 28 
days — a clean 364-day year. Lustre completes exactly one full cycle 
per month, and the days of the week are named for the seven moons.

The calendar below tracks the current campaign date and marks known 
events as they become relevant.
