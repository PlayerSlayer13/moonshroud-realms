# The Seven Moons of Orb

The world of Orb turns beneath seven moons. Every person who has ever 
looked up at a clear night sky in the Moonshroud Realms has seen them — 
some bright, some faint, some moving faster than others across the dark. 
Most people know them as a fact of life, the way they know the seasons 
or the tides. Scholars and druids know them as something more.

## What Is Known

The seven moons are not merely lights. Each has its own influence, its 
own rhythm, and its own consequence for the world below. Sailors read 
them for navigation. Farmers watch them for planting cycles. Druids 
study the patterns of their conjunctions and oppositions as carefully as 
any text. Astrologers argue endlessly about what each alignment means.

The **Circle of the Seven Moons** was a druidic order devoted entirely 
to mapping the rhythms of Orb's seven satellites and interpreting the 
tides of magic they cast across the world. Their knowledge was deep and 
carefully accumulated over generations. Much of it has not survived in 
accessible form.

What is commonly understood is this: when multiple moons align, 
something in the world responds. Magic behaves differently. Certain 
creatures grow restless. The boundaries between Orb and the mirror 
planes — the Umbral and Haelo — grow thinner or thicker depending on 
which moons are prominent. Whether this is a property of the moons 
themselves or of what lies behind them is a question scholars have not 
settled.

## What Remains Unknown

The full names, individual properties, and specific influences of each 
of the seven moons are knowledge that has not yet fully reached the 
party. This page will expand as the campaign progresses and more is 
learned.

There are people in the world who know more. Finding them is another 
matter.
